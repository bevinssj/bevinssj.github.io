  • Baby's first math paper: Strong Homotopy Lie Algebras and Hypergraphs.

Let \(G\) be a simple finite graph. the 2-step nilpotent Lie algebra \(\mathcal L(G)\) is given by the underlying vector space \[ \mathcal L(G) = V \oplus \bigwedge^2 V/W\] where \(V = \text{span}_{\mathbb R}\{x_i\mid i \in V(G)\}\) and \(W = \text{span}_{\mathbb R}\{x_i\wedge x_j \mid \{i,j\} \notin E(G)\}\).
